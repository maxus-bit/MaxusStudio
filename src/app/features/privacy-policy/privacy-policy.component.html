Maxus Studio Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

We, the Maxus Studio team ("We," "Our," or "Service"), take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our web application, Maxus Studio (the "Application").

By using our Service, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data to operate the Service:

1.1. Personal Data

When you register and use the Application, we may collect:

  • Email Address: To create an account, sign in (via Email or Google), and restore access.
  • Profile Data: Username and profile picture (avatar), if provided via Google Auth or uploaded by you.

1.2. Content Data

  • Text Queries (Prompts): The text you enter to generate images. These are processed to enhance quality before generation.
  • Images: Generated images, as well as images you upload as references (Image-to-Image feature).
  • Chat History: The history of your queries and generations is saved for your convenience.

1.3. Usage Data and Technical Data

  • IP address, browser type, operating system version.
  • Error logs and usage statistics (number of credits spent).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Providing and supporting the Service (image generation).
  • Managing your account and synchronizing generation history.
  • Processing payments and managing subscriptions (credit/gen accounting).
  • Sending important notifications (e.g., password resets or email confirmations).
  • Improving the quality of the App.

3. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We do not sell your data. However, to operate the App, we use trusted third-party providers:

  • Google Firebase (US): We use Firebase for user authentication (Auth), database storage (Firestore), and file storage (Storage). Your data is stored on secure Google servers.
    Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Cloud Vertex AI (US): We use Google's generative AI models (Gemini and Imagen) to process your text prompts and generate images. Your prompts and uploaded reference images are transmitted to Google Cloud solely for the purpose of generating content.
    Google Cloud AI Data Governance
  • Stripe (US): (Future): For payment processing. We do not store your full bank card details. This processing is handled by Stripe.
    Stripe Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

  • User passwords are stored encrypted (hashed) by Firebase.
  • Data transmission between your browser and our servers (including API servers) is protected by SSL encryption (HTTPS).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and Correction: You can view and edit your profile data in the App settings.
  • Data Deletion: You can delete individual chats and images from your history. You can also completely delete your account via "Settings" -> "Danger Zone." Deleting your account will permanently delete all your personal data and chat history from our database.

6. Use of Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your session (so you don't have to enter your password each time).
  • Storage your preferences (such as your selected interface language).

7. Children

Our Service is not intended for individuals under 13 years of age (or other legal age required by law in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
